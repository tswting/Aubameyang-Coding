AUBAMEYANG

Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang has won the Premier League Player of the Month award for October. The Gabon international scored five times in five matches, as well as contributing an assist. He memorably struck a brace in our 3-1 win over Leicester City at Emirates Stadium.
